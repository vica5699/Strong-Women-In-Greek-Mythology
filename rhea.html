Rhea, a Titan, daughter of Uranus and Gaea, Mother of the Gods.

Rhea's Story


Rhea was responsible for helping things flow within her kingdom.
She was also given the title "mother of gods"


Rhea may not be a very familiar female character in Greek mythology, likely because she is a Titan rather than a goddess.
However, this does not mean that she is not an important figure.
She was given the titles that were previously mentioned for a reason.
She assisted Zeus when he needed help and was certainly very important to everyone to be given the "mother of gods" title.
