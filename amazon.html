Amazon Women The Amazon Women were a tribal group from Greek mythology that would fight and go to war to protect their land and their people.



Hippolyta, Queen of the Amazons

"Brief Story about Hippolyta Hippolyta was very important in helping Heracles fulfill his twelve labours.
These were a set of tasks that Heracles was given by Eurystheus in the hope that he would be granted immortality.
One of the tasks required him to get an object from Hippolyta.
Hippolyta noticed Heracles's bravery and willingly gave the object to him.





This story shows that Hippolyta, although a brave queen of the Amazons was also an understanding woman.
She recognized willpower and strength in others and was willing to show them compassion.

Battles of the Amazon

The Amazons were forced to eventually fight Heracles and other important figures in Greek mythology.
Although they were defeated in a few of these battles, the Amazons never stopped fighting for their people.
